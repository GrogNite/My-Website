Microlighting in the UK

Microlighting offers a fun, affordable route into the world of recreational aviation. In the UK, you can choose from two main types:

Types of Microlight

Fixed Wing A mini airplane. 3 axis control using stick and rudder controls.

Flex Wing A powered hanglider, uses weightshift control. Akin to a motorcycle in the sky.

Why Microlight?

Cost-effective:

Licence training costs about half of a full PPL, and ongoing flight costs are much lower.

Accessible:

Most healthy adults can fly (similar to driving requirements), and medical demands are low.

Great community:

There's a vibrant scene of clubs, shared trips, and even championships - often flying to France or other parts of Europe.
